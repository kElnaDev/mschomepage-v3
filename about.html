What is the homepage?

The homepage of Manly Selective Campus was created to allow the students, teachers and other staff of the school to easily navigate the various resources and websites associated with and used by the school.

How do I use the homepage?

On computer / laptop, hover over and click the home icon in the navigation bar on the left.

On mobile, tap the home icon in the navigation bar at the bottom of the screen.

Clicking this icon will take you to the Home section. There, find the relevant button or use the search feature to quickly search the website and the rest of the internet.
